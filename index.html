06 / FAQ

Common
questions.

How long does ceramic coating last? +

Depending on the grade, our ceramic coatings last from 1 year up to 5+ years when properly maintained. We offer several tiers to match your budget and how long you plan to keep the car.

Will PPF yellow over time? +

Cheap, import-grade PPF can yellow within months. Authentic top-tier PPF is UV-stabilized and optically clear — we install only products with manufacturer-backed non-yellowing warranties.

Can you do this on a brand-new car? +

Absolutely — in fact, right after delivery is the ideal moment. Fresh paint, minimal contamination, and maximum protection from day one. Book a slot before picking up the car if possible.

How long will my car be with you? +

A ceramic coating with correction typically takes 2–3 days. Full PPF installs 3–5 days depending on coverage. Premium carwash or tint can often be same-day. We'll quote exact turnaround after inspection.

Do you service customers from Manila / QC / nearby cities? +

Yes — we regularly welcome clients driving in from Bulacan, Metro Manila, Pampanga, and nearby provinces. Book ahead via WhatsApp so we can reserve a bay and minimize your waiting time.

What payment methods do you accept? +

Cash, GCash, and bank transfers. For higher-tier PPF and ceramic packages, message us to discuss installment options.
